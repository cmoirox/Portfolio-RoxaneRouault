This project marks the completion of an end-of-studies thesis, delving into the fascinating intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and digital creation. The thesis is a comprehensive exploration of the theory and practical applications of AI in the digital creation landscape, as well as its prospective future developments. The project is segmented into three primary phases: Reflection, Design, and Layout.
