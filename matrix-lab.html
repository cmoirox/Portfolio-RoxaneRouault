Matrix_Lab - 2023

Photo & 3D Compositing

Creation of a series of experiments exploring 3D and the incorporation of real photoshoots. Use of Blender software for 3D rendering, texturing, lighting... Post-production and editing in Photoshop. The ambiance was meant to represent a dark, dystopian, and technological universe.

Composition on the development of my work, from photography to 3D to retouching.
