FontKroma AI - 2023

End of Studies Project

FontKroma is a platform concept that uses AI for the creation of typographic characters. The main objective of this platform is to democratize typography for a wider audience through Artificial Intelligence. To do this, the platform allows users to generate, edit, learn about, and share typographic characters. For this project, I had to use my programming skills: developing an AI model, and in artistic direction: visual identity, motion design, UI UX.

The result of several typographies obtained after multiple iterations of my AI model, which utilizes a generative model called CGAN.

User interface of the FontKroma Typographic AI and an AI-generated character alphabet.

Screen of the script for the neural networks of FontKroma, featuring a generator network and a discriminator network. Essential for designing a CGAN model.

Creation of the interface design for the FontKroma website & motion design to promote the product.
